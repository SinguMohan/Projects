Q8 e-tron Specifications

Electric. Among other wonderful things

The Audi e-tron has fulfilled its pioneering role as an all-electric vehicle. Now, the time has come to include it by name in the portfolio of Audi models as the Audi Q8 e-tron.

Specifications

  • Charged from 10% to up to 80% in 31 min³ at maximum DC charging power⁴
  • Range (WLTP), up to 582 km
  • Maximum DC charging power, up to 170 kW⁴

Power consumption (combined¹) in kWh/100 km: 24.4-20.6; CO₂ emissions (combined¹) in g/km: 0.
