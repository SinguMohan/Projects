S5 Sportsback Specifications

Powerful and attractive.
The Audi S5 Sportback.

The S5 Sportback offers plenty of space and the power of a sports car. An enlarged interior and a high-power engine make this five-door vehicle into something very special. What makes the S5 Sportback such an impressive sight are its design language and S-specific exterior elements.

What makes the big picture? Every detail

The Audi S5 Sportback features various design elements that emphasize its sporty nature. S-specific bumpers, a radiator grille with a honeycomb design in titanium black, and wide air inlets in the frame with a blade in matte aluminum silver. The 48.26 cm alloy wheels, S-sport suspension, and mirror housings in aluminum finish add further sporty touches. The rear boasts a horizontal trim surrounding the S diffuser, S-specific twin tailpipes, and an S-rear spoiler, emphasizing the car's athletic stance.
