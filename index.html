Audi is a German luxury car manufacturer that originated in the 19th century. The company's name comes from the Latin word audi, which means "to listen". The Audi logo is made up of four interlocking rings, which represent the four companies that merged to form Auto Union, Audi's predecessor.

Audi is considered a pioneer in the field of all-wheel drive technology. Tagged Quattro, the company's AWD tech trickled down to road-going Audi cars after decades of fine-tuning and winning in rallying.

Audi cars are known for their refined design, high performance, and cutting-edge technology. Audi’s unique features include adaptive cruise control, virtual cockpit, and the Quattro all-wheel drive system, offering enhanced traction and control.
